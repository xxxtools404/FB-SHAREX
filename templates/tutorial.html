How to Get Cookies

Use these extensions: Kiwi/Firefox Extension

Step 1: Install the Extension

Download and install the extension from the link above for Kiwi or Firefox. Follow the installation prompts in your browser.

Step 2: Extract Cookies

Open Facebook in your browser, log in to your account, and use the extension to copy your cookies. Paste the cookies (as a string) into the admin panel under "Cookies" (one per line). Ensure the cookies are valid to avoid errors during sharing.

Note: Cookies expire, so refresh them regularly. If extraction fails, check the extension's documentation.
